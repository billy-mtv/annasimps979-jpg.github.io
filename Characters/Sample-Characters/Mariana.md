# Mariana de la Luna

**Concept:** Mariana is the daughter of High Priestess de la Luna of the Church of the Eclipse.  At 19, she is set to inherit the position from her mother next year.  All she has to do is complete her final pilgrimage, and then be consecrated in a private ceremony.  With her bodyguards behind her, what could possibly go wrong?

**Aspiration:** To be just like her mother.

**Relationships:** Faith in the Eclipse, Loyalty to her Mother, Fear of the Star Nomads
