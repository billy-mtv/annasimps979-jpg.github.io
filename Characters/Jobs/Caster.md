# Caster

Casters specialize in magical attacks.

While they are a Caster, characters gain the following bonuses to their statistics:

*  Their Resistance is increased by an amount equal to their Body.
*  Their Magical Attack is increased by an amount equal to their Mind.
*  Their Magical Defense is increased by an amount equal to their Spirit.

Abilities by Rank:

**Rank 1:** Magic Missile. Ranged. Cost: 3 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless magical attack against a single target.

**Rank 2:** Elemental Bolt. Ranged. Cost: 5 MP, 1 AP.  Make a magical attack against a single target whose elemental type is the same as your base elemental type.

**Rank 3:** Trained Spellcaster. Passive.  Flex powers that are magical attacks cost 5 MP instead of 10 MP.

**Rank 4:** Magic Chain. Special. Cost: 8 MP, 1 AP.  Make a ranged, colorless magical attack against a single target.  If you break the target's Shell, you may use this ability again against another target without spending any MP or AP.

**Rank 5:** Magic Burst. Ranged. Cost: 7 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless magical attack against a single target.  On a hit, the attack deals 2x damage in addition to other modifiers.

**Rank 6:** Elemental Burst. Ranged. Cost: 9 MP, 1 AP.  Make a magical attack against a single target whose elemental type is equal to your elemental type.  On a hit, the attack deals 2x damage in addition to other modifiers.

**Rank 7:** Elemental Chain. Special. Cost: 10 MP, 1 AP.  Make a ranged magical attack against a single enemy whose elemntal type is your base elemental type.  If you break the target's Shell, you may use this ability again against another target without spending any MP or AP.

**Rank 8:** Magic Storm. Multi. Cost: 13 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless magical attack against all enemies on the battlefield.

**Rank 9:** Elemental Storm. Multi. Cost: 15 MP, 1 AP.  Make a magical attack against all enemies on the battlefield that has the same elemental type as your base elemental type.

**Capstone:** Apocalypse. Multi. Cost: 1 Anima, 2 AP.  Make a magical attack against all enemies on the battlefield that has the same elemental type as your base elemental type.  On a hit, it deals 3x damage in addition to other multipliers.
