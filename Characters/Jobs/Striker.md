# Striker

The Striker specializes in physical attacks.

While they are a Striker, characters gain the following bonuses to their statistics:
*  Their Physical Attack is increased by an amount equal to their Body.
*  Their Evasion is increased by an amount equal to their Mind.
*  Their Physical Defense is increased by an amount equal to their Spirit.

## Abilities by Rank

**Rank 1:** Strike. Melee. Cost: 3 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless physical attack that deals 1.5x damage on a hit in addition to other modifiers.

**Rank 2:** Elemental Attack. Melee. Cost: 3 MP, 1 AP.  Make a physical attack whose elemental type is the same as your base elemental type.

**Rank 3:** Power Attack. Melee. Cost: 5 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless physical attack.  On a hit, this attack's damage is multiplied by 2 in addition to other modifiers.

**Rank 4:** Cleave. Multi. Cost: 7 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless, melee physical attack against all enemies in the front row.

**Rank 5:** Leaping Strike. Ranged. Cost: 5 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless physical attack against a single target.

**Rank 6:** Elemental Cleave. Multi. Cost: 10 MP, 1 AP.  Make a melee physical attack against all enemies in the front row whose elemental type is the same as your base elemental type.

**Rank 7:** Whirlwind. Multi. Cost: 12 MP, 1 AP.  Make a colorless physical attack against all enemies on the battlefield.

**Rank 8:** Elemental Leap. Ranged. Cost: 8 MP, 1 AP.  Make a physical attack against a single target whose elemental type is the same as your base elemental type.

**Rank 9:** Elemental Whirlwind. Multi. Cost: 15 MP, 1 AP.  Make a physical attack against all enemies on the battlefield whose elemental type is the same as your base elemental type.

**Capstone:** Dragonslayer. Melee. Cost: 1 Anima, 2 AP.  Makes a colorless physical attack against a single target.  If the target's HP is at maximum, the attack's damage is multiplied by 4 in addition to other modifiers.  Otherwise, it is multiplied by 2.
